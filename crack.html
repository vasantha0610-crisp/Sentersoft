Safety Tips

Don't store firecrackers near burning candles and diyas. Don't light firecrackers in narrow by lanes; preferably use open areas and parks. Don't wear synthetic clothing; preferably wear thick cotton clothing. Don't wear loosely hanging clothes; secure all clothes properly.
